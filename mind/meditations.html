About

Below we provide a series of guided meditations. The meditations are designed to take you from a complete beginner to intermediate practices. You are welcome to use these meditaions as much as you like. Do not feel any pressure to move forward if you don't feel ready.

If you are looking for more or ours don't quite seem to be there right fit, check out Medito. Medito Foundation is a nonprofit dedicated to improving mental wellbeing and helping people cope better with depression, stress, anxiety, and any other negative states of mind. All their meditations are free and wonderful.

Introduction

This session marks the beginning of the Introduction Course, designed to introduce you to the basics of mindfulness meditation and aims to assist you in establishing a consistent meditation routine.You will start with short sessions and slowly build to full 10 minute sessions. Each session will teach you something, so be sure not to skip any, though you are welcome to do multiple session in a day (just remember it's not a race).
