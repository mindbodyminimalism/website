Below is a list of resources you may use to help you in your learning journey. The sources are meant to help you find a starting points and motivation when learning a new topic. There are no rules when it comes to choosing something to learn. You may find it benefitial to choose something that aids in you in your career or something that coincides with another pillar of Mind Body Minimalism. Whatever your reasons are, learning should be both challenging and rewarding experience.

If you are a college student, thinking of switching careers or simply want to know how to maximize the your career you are currently working in consider checking out 80000Hours.org

"80,000 Hours" is a nonprofit organization that focuses on helping individuals make the most of their careers to have a positive impact on the world. The name "80,000 Hours" represents the estimated amount of time an individual spends in their career, which is a significant opportunity for making a difference.
